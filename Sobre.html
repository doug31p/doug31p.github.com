Biography

Douglas Santos is a master's student in Geographic Information Systems and spatial planning at the University of Porto. He has experience, in processing satellite images to detect areas with potential for lithium exploration in Brazil, with 1 article published on this topic. During his professional career as an intern at the Municipality of São Paulo, between 2014 and 2016, where remote sensing is used to detect and assess deforestation sites and invasions of municipal green fields, using techniques to georeference and monitor the health of trees under the responsibility of the County . He graduated in Environmental Engineering from the University of São Paulo (UNICID), where he also graduated in Environmental Management.
